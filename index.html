Dangerous Jungle

Description

2D platform browser game based on Google T-Rex.

Controls

Single Player:

AD or left/right arrows to move; W or up arrow to jump;

Dual Mode:

Player 1 (Jungle Man) : left/right/up arrows to move and jump
Player 2 : AD to move and W to jump. Shift to attack

Instructions
Single Player:
Survive the enemies onslaught by using arrow keys and spacebar to dodge.

Dual Mode:
Despite being hidden from view, you still get discover by a ferocious enemy (controlled by your friend). Try to survive its attacks and find a way to beat him
