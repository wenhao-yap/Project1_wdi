Dangerous Jungle

Description

2D platform browser game based on Google T-Rex.

Controls
AD or left/right arrows to move; W or up arrow to jump; Shift to attack(alpha)
Instructions
Survive the enemies onslaught by using arrow keys and spacebar to dodge.
